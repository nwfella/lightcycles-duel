HOW TO DUEL

The Grid

Each player controls a lightcycle that leaves a deadly light trail. Crash into any trail or wall and you're derezzed!

Diagonal Movement

Unlike classic Tron, you can move in 8 directions! Tilt the joystick in any direction — the bike follows.

Tips

  • Cut corners — Diagonal moves let you trap opponents
  • Watch the trails — Yours and theirs. Don't get boxed in
  • Be unpredictable — Change direction often

Controls

  • Joystick — Drag to steer your lightcycle
  • Tap — Start the match from the countdown
